🚀 Welcome to the Future of AI

The Onicai team hosted their first-ever X Space, and the turnout was electric. With a global audience tuning in, the founders took us behind the scenes of their bold mission: Build the world’s first truly decentralized AI ecosystem — from the many, for the many.

🌍 Why Decentralized AI Matters

"We don’t want AI to come from a few centralized entities while the rest of us are just consumers." – Patrick

The team laid out a clear case:

  • 🛡️ Sovereignty: Own your AI stack end-to-end
  • 🧩 Transparency: Know what model runs, when, and how
  • 🤝 Collaboration: Enable agents that can work together

📜 The Onicai Origin Story

Each founder shared their path:

  • Arjaan began by porting conversational AI to ICP and built the first C++ CDK (ICPP-Pro)
  • Patrick ran LLMs inside browsers for full user-side privacy
  • Nuno brought in years of UI/UX experience to design fluid decentralized apps

Together, they formed Onicai in 2023. Since then, they’ve shipped:

  • 🎨 ICPP-Art NFTs (funded bootcamps)
  • 🧠 Charles AI NFT (world’s first on-chain AI storyteller)
  • 🔗 Proof of AI Work Protocol (PoAIW)
  • 📜 IConfucius — a fully autonomous LLM agent that lives and runs on-chain

🧘 About IConfucius

"It’s not just a bot. It’s a living example of what's possible with on-chain AI." – Arjaan

Powered by Qwen 2.5B LLM via llama.cpp, running entirely in a canister:

  • Generates quotes and posts to X and Odin.fun
  • Autonomous and open-source
  • True sovereignty vs centralized APIs like Elna/OpenAI

⚙️ What Is FunnAI?

"Imagine if Bitcoin miners were AI agents solving real challenges — that’s FunnAI." – Patrick

Onicai’s upcoming protocol is built on Proof of AI Work:

  • 🧠 Spin up an AI miner (agent) with one click
  • 🛠️ Solves AI tasks autonomously
  • 🎯 Earns $FunnAI token based on performance
  • 🎓 Backed by a $50K Dfinity grant

💎 Token Strategy

  • ✅ IConfucius Token – Live on Odin.fun (Bitcoin Rune)
  • 🚫 FunnAI Token – No presale. Minted gradually via agent work
  • 🎁 Early Supporters: Charles AI NFT, ICPP-Art, IConfucius holders get benefits
